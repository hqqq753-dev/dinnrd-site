Questions

How much does it cost?

$5 a month or $48 a year. The first 500 people on the waitlist lock in that rate as their beta tester price. No price hikes, no surprises.

Is there a free trial?

The first 500 people on the waitlist get their first 3 months free. After that, your beta tester pricing kicks in and stays there.

What happens after I join the waitlist?

We'll email you the moment TestFlight opens. If you're in the first 500, you get 3 months free to try it properly, then $5/mo after that. Your beta tester price locks in the moment you sign up.

How do I add things to my pantry?

Mostly by talking to it. Say something like "we have three tins of tomatoes and a block of cheddar" and Dinnrd parses it straight in. You can also type manually if you prefer.

Can I save my own recipes?

Yes. Dinnrd has a cookbooks section where you can save, organize, and search your own recipes. Anything you add goes straight into the meal planning rotation.

What platforms is Dinnrd on?

iOS only right now. Android is on the roadmap.

Is my data private?

Your meal plans, pantry, and grocery lists live on your account. We don't sell your data. Full details in our privacy policy.
