FAQ

How much is Dinnrd Pro?

$5 a month or $48 per year. Everyone who onboards Dinnrd during the beta phase will lock in these rates for life.

Is there a free trial?

The first 500 to download & onboard Dinnrd get 3 months free. You also earn 1 additional free month for every paying Dinnrd Pro referral, up to 12 months total. After that, your $5/mo (or $48/yr) beta rate kicks in — and stays locked for life.

How do I claim my 3 months free?

Your 3 free months are locked in when you download Dinnrd and finish onboarding, not when you join the waitlist. We will open TestFlight to waitlist members in the order you joined, so the earlier you signed up, the better your chances.

What happens after I join the waitlist?

We'll email you the moment TestFlight opens for Dinnrd, in the order you joined the waitlist. If you're one of the first 500 to download & onboard, you will receive 3 months of Dinnrd Pro for free. Then $5/mo for life and a beta tester profile-certification.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your Dinnrd Pro subscription anytime from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Does Dinnrd work on Android?

Not yet. Dinnrd launches on iOS first, with Android coming next. Join the waitlist either way and we'll tell you when it's your turn.

Can the whole household use Dinnrd?

Yes. Dinnrd lets you create a household and invite your family members so everyone can contribute to the week's plan. All household members share the same pantry, grocery list, and meal plan. The best part: you only need a single Dinnrd Pro subscription for the whole household to have full access.

How do I add things to my pantry?

Mostly by talking to it. Say something like "three cans of tomatoes, some mozzarella, and half a kilo of chicken that's going off in two days" and Dinnrd figures out the rest. You can type manually if you prefer.

Can I save my own recipes?

Yes. Dinnrd has a built-in cookbook where you can save, organize, and search your own recipes. Anything you add can show up in your meal plan.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your meal plans, pantry, and grocery lists live on your account. Full details in our privacy policy.
